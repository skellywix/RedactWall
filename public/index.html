How it works

Prompts to AI tools are intercepted at the network gateway, scanned for PII, and blocked if they breach policy. They sit here until a Security Admin approves or denies. Approved prompts are released to the AI service; denied prompts never leave the network. Every action is written to a tamper-evident audit log.
